`APICAL MERISTEM`

 - `APICAL MERISTEM`: 
         - In **shoots** (Buds) & 
         - **root tips**`

Cell division.
Zone of elongation – cells elongate giving growth.
Zone of differentiation/maturation – layers of tissues form.
Root hairs – produce a vast surface area for water and mineral uptake.
