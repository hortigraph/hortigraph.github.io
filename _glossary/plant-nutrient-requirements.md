`PLANT NUTRIENT REQUIREMENTS`

`

Nitrogen   N;
Phosphorus (Phosphates)   P2O5;
Potassium   K2O;
(commonly referred to as N.P.K.);
Calcium;
Magnesium.

Minor (trace) nutrients:
Iron;
The following are trace elements, : Manganese, Boron, Copper, Zinc, Molybdenum, Sulphur, (Chlorine Sodium).
